Cash Back Policy

At Viratrades LTD, we understand the importance of maximizing the value of your investments. That’s why we offer a cash back policy for certain investment products. This policy allows our clients to earn cash back on their investments, providing an additional source of returns.

Our cash back policy is available for select investment products, such as mutual funds and exchange-traded funds (ETFs). When clients invest in these products, they may be eligible to receive a portion of the management fees paid by the fund company. The amount of cash back varies by product and is subject to change.

We believe that our cash back policy is a unique and valuable benefit for our clients. By earning cash back on their investments, clients can increase their overall returns and potentially achieve their financial goals faster.

In addition to our cash back policy, we also offer a range of other benefits to our clients. These include low fees, access to a broad range of investment products, and personalized investment advice from our team of experienced professionals.

We believe that investing should be accessible and affordable for everyone, which is why we strive to keep our fees low. We offer transparent fee structures and no hidden costs, so our clients can invest with confidence.

Our clients also have access to a wide variety of investment products, including mutual funds, ETFs, individual stocks, bonds, and more. Our team of investment professionals carefully selects and monitors each product, ensuring that our clients have access to high-quality investment options.

Finally, we believe that personalized advice is essential for successful investing. Our team of financial advisors and investment professionals work with our clients to understand their unique needs and goals, and develop customized investment strategies to help them achieve those goals.

At Viratrades LTD, our commitment to providing value to our clients is at the core of everything we do. Our cash back policy is just one example of how we strive to help our clients maximize their investments and achieve financial success.
